My Passion for Medical Device Design and Why Stryker is the Perfect Fit

I pursued a career in mechanical engineering with the desire to work on life-saving medical devices. The opportunity to join Stryker as a Design Engineer, specifically focusing on designing patient-specific craniomaxillofacial implants, aligns perfectly with this aspiration.

As a recent graduate of Purdue University with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and a minor in Design & Innovation, combined with hands-on experience in the highly regulated medical industry, I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to Stryker’s mission of improving patient outcomes through innovative medical device design.

During my internships at AbbVie and Pfizer, I gained valuable experience in safety, testing, and attention to detail in developing critical technologies. At AbbVie, I performed HVAC system analysis and utilized AutoCAD for design tasks. My work at Pfizer involved revising P&ID drawings and assisting with equipment re-qualification. Additionally, in my senior design capstone project, my team and I designed and built an industrial product using Creo. These experiences have provided me with a solid foundation in mechanical systems and a meticulous approach to product design, skills that are essential for creating customized implants at Stryker.

My technical expertise is further strengthened by my experience in leading and collaborating with cross-functional teams. As the Student Activities Committee Head for Purdue Mechanical Engineering Ambassadors, I led a team in organizing events for the School of Mechanical Engineering. Additionally, I instructed a course titled How Stuff Works, where I simplified complex engineering concepts for freshman students. These roles have honed my leadership, communication, and organizational abilities, preparing me to excel in a collaborative environment like Stryker’s.

I am genuinely excited about the prospect of joining Stryker’s team and contributing to the development of patient-specific solutions like the PEEK and MEDPOR Customized Implants. I am confident that my skills and experiences align well with the requirements of the Design Engineer position, and I look forward to discussing how I can add value to your team.

Thank you for considering my application.
